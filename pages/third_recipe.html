Traveller, first recipe is - Herbal beverage!

    Ingredients:
  • Water
  • Any plants(from which you want to create a beverage)
    • Everything below is optional:
  • (3-inch) cinnamon stick
  • Sugar, or honey, to taste
  • Dried fruit, fresh fruit, nuts
Steps to Make It:
 You can make herbal beverages from many plants.
Tasty drinks can be had from young leaves of stinging nettle, mint,howthorn, raspberries and strawberries, and on flowers of linden, hawthorn or chamomile.
The drink is made by placing flowers or leaves in boiling water and letting it cook for a couple of minutes.
